Post #3 - 10.10.2020

It's 6:55 AM right now. Early right? Well, Tigey is awake and active. Why? Because he's nocturnal! Meaning that he sleeps in the daytime and is active in the night! He has already eaten another gallon of jelly beans and also 5,000,000,000 bowls of mashed potatoes and spinach. Oh, did I not mention that his favorite food is mashed potatoes and spinach? And did you know he, even though he's almost as big as my torso, is a unicelluar organism! Unicelluar means the living thing(organism) only has one cell! A cell is defined as the smallest structural and functional unit of an organism. Basically the building block of all living things. I'll see you soon! Byeeeee!

Post #4 - 10.11.2020

Tigey went to the Grand Canyon today! Above, you can see him in front of a view point. We took many pictures of him. The Grand Canyon is home to elk, mule deer, rattlesnakes, and mountain lions. Do you know what Tigey would do if facing off with an apex predator ( a predator at the top of a food chain that is not preyed upon by any other animal)? He would try to make friends with the predator! Even in a dangerous situation, he loves to make friends and is very generous.
