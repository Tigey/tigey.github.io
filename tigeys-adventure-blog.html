Post #3 - 10.10.2020

It's 6:55 AM right now. Early right? Well, Tigey is awake and active. Why? Because he's nocturnal! Meaning that he sleeps in the daytime and is active in the night! He has already eaten another gallon of jelly beans and also 5,000,000,000 bowls of mashed potatoes and spinach. Oh, did I not mention that his favorite food is mashed potatoes and spinach. And did you know he, even though he's almost as big as my torso, is a unicelluar organism! Unicelluar means the living thing(organism) only has one cell! A cell is defined as the smallest structural and functional unit of an organism. Basically the building block of all living things. In a more simple sense, if you are building a lego peice set, then the cell would be the plastic that makes the LEGOS. I'll see you soon! Byeeeee!
